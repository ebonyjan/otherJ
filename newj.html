Welcome!

Welcome to Joon! My name is Ebony. I am 19 years old and very excited to officially start my web development journey. I am not a creative person and find it very difficult to think of things on my own. By making this page about my love for music, I am hoping to be more creative, practice, and experiment with what I have learned.

Browse the Home page, Search by Genre, or look at your profile. In the Home page, you will find the weekly newsletter that will include at least four different songs from different genres and artists. This will be a good opportunity to discover new songs, re-discover old favorites, or see what others think about a song you like.

Look up songs under the Genre tab or go to your profile to build your playlists and add more songs.

About Me

I created Joon for people to share some of their playlists with others and have a space to express themselves. Who are your favorite artists? What is your favorite genre of music? What songs do you listen to when you are happy, sad, upset? What artists do others with similar tastes listen to? What are some of your favorite lyrics? Music is a big part of our lives. Sometimes we have memories attached to certain songs, or a specific feeling. This is a place to share that with others, or just have it for yourself to personalize.

Web Development Journey

When I was in high school, I started to get very interested in learning about web development. Out of all the jobs I was interested in, being a web developer interested me the most and I was certain I wouldn't get bored of it quickly. Starting this class has been very exciting and rewarding so far. Creating tables has been so fun for me to learn. Creating tables in HTML is very different from creating one for a powerpoint or a document, but they are so interesting and can be used for many things. So far I have learned about tags, images, embedded videos, favicons, different types of headings, lists, checkboxes, including links and getting them to open in a different browser, and many other things. They can seem like simple things, but it is interesting learning about all the work that makes up a website.
